Strangewaves is for sale!

A very cute 1978 shoal keel Tanzer 7.5 well loved

Strangewaves is a very capable small cuiser/liveabord and a great simple boat to get started. I lived onbord for a year in Victoria but also took her along with my husband to several places between the San Juan islands, Puget Sound, Gulf islands, Discovery islands and more.

The Tanzer 7.5 has a good reputation as a tough little boat. She sleeps four people but is a good beginning cruiser for two. Well loved and maintained, she features:

  • A very reliable 2012 Yamaha 9.9 HP with an excellent engine mount, an alternator for charging the batteries, an electric and hand start option, a main fuel tank of 5 Gallon + 6 extra gallons of storage.
  • A Standard Horizon Chartplotter with AIS (CPF 190i), a VHF with AIS (GX2150) and a mast mounted antenna.
  • A fairly recent main sail from UK sails with two reefing points.
  • Two foresails: one small jib with reef point in excellent condition (great in high winds) and a larger genoa, old but very usable.
  • Two whisker poles.
  • An electrical system of 30 Amp with two 15 amps breakers, two 12V batteries incliding an isolated starter battery. Both appear to be in good condition.
  • A properly sized denthforth anchor with 80 feet of chain recently replaced that we have anchored on countless times.
  • A small capable kitchen that include an icebox, small sink with new hand Whale Pump, a portable single butane burner and ample food storage.
  • survey done in early 2015.
  • Two large lazzarets (stern lockers).
  • Bottom work completed in the fall of 2016.
  • Disconected Jabsco head included.
  • A disco ball.
